ABOUT US

Surefire is a community organization comprising of like-minded individuals who came together for a singular mutual purpose: to pull resources and build wealth for its members. It was formed in late 2020 and those who chose to join and remained with the group subscribed to the core values and aspirations of the organization for the mutual benefit of its members.

Surefire LLC is a legally incorporated community association organized under the laws of the State of Maryland and formed for the mutual benefit of its members.

OUR MISSION

“We aim to build mutual wealth in a strategically-focused manner where returns consistently out-pace investment.”
